## Classes




### `ContentOrchestrator`


Handles content and asset discovery.

Responsibilities:
    - Discover content (pages and sections)
    - Discover assets (site and theme)
    - Set up page/section references for navigation
    - Apply cascading frontmatter from sections to pages
